Best AI Marketing Tools in 2026

AI marketing tools help businesses and creators create content, write ads, improve SEO, analyze campaigns, and grow faster.

Quick Picks

  • Best for SEO: Surfer SEO
  • Best for marketing teams: Jasper
  • Best general marketing assistant: ChatGPT
  • Best for copy: Copy.ai
  • Best CRM ecosystem: HubSpot AI

1. Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO helps with content optimization, SEO briefs, keyword planning, and search-focused writing.

2. Jasper

Jasper is built for marketing teams that need branded content, campaigns, and repeatable workflows.

3. ChatGPT

ChatGPT is flexible for content ideas, ad copy, email drafts, SEO outlines, and customer research.

4. Copy.ai

Copy.ai is useful for sales copy, ad ideas, social media copy, and marketing workflows.

5. HubSpot AI

HubSpot AI is useful for businesses already using HubSpot for CRM, marketing, sales, and customer operations.

Final Verdict

For SEO and content marketing, Surfer SEO and ChatGPT are excellent starting points.
