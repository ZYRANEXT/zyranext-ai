Best AI Video Editors in 2026

AI video editors help creators save time by generating captions, cutting clips, improving audio, turning scripts into videos, and creating social media content faster.

Quick Picks

  • Best for short videos: CapCut
  • Best for text-based editing: Descript
  • Best for AI video generation: Runway
  • Best for business videos: Synthesia
  • Best for repurposing content: Pictory

1. CapCut

CapCut is beginner-friendly and strong for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, captions, templates, and fast mobile editing.

2. Descript

Descript is useful for editing video by editing text. It works well for podcasts, tutorials, interviews, and talking-head videos.

3. Runway

Runway focuses on AI video generation, background removal, creative effects, and advanced visual editing.

4. Synthesia

Synthesia is built for business videos, training content, AI avatars, presentations, and explainers.

5. Pictory

Pictory can turn scripts, blog posts, and text prompts into videos. It is useful for marketers and content repurposing.

Final Verdict

For most creators, CapCut is the easiest starting point. For serious workflows, Descript and Runway are stronger options.
